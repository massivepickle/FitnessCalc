C Y C L I N G

(Click here/scroll down to calculate an estimate of how many calories you burned while cycling)

Cycling is one of the best ways to increase heigh, as there's a lot of stretching involved. It is a great way to gradually increase your stamina.
It helps strengthen your leg muscles in particular, however, it's not only limited to the legs.

STAMINA: If you don't have much stamina, start off with doing 4-5km. Gradually increase to 10km, and later even to 20+km if doable. Don't care much about the speed till you are able to easily do 10km. Doing an overall slow-medium speed is good enough. You can gradually increase it.

STRENGTH: The higher the gear, the more force would be required to revolve the pedal at the same speed. If the track is long without many turns, higher gears will be very helpful as they give much more speed per pedal revolution, hence travelling the same distance quicker.

HEIGHT: It's very important to cycle EVERYDAY in order to increase height. It's fine to leave 2-5 days a month, but you must be regular to observe an increase in your height.
